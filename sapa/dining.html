Stunning food with views to match

Tickle your taste buds in one of our four restaurants, offering fine Vietnamese cuisine with touches of the northern highlands and European fare made possible by fresh, local produce. From comfortable and casual to high-end elegance, our restaurants and eateries offer something for every mood.

Olivio Restaurant

lunch:11:00 AM – 2:00 PM (Fridays & Saturdays)

dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM (Nightly)

location:Main building

Olivio is where we serve sophisticated European cuisine, a blend of Italian recipes with Mediterranean sensibilities. On cool evenings you can sit outside on the terrace surrounded by flowers and greenery.
