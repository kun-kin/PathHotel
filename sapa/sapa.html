Welcome to our mountain resort, perched on a private spot atop one of Sapa’s highest hills. Here time stands still, giving you ample opportunities to stroll through the flower gardens and absorb captivating views of the surrounding mountain range. During colonial times Sapa – surrounded by what is referred to as the “Vietnamese Alps” – was a hill station where the French escaped the Hanoi heat, and this heritage is reflected in our elegant, regal interior design and architecture.
ACCOMMODATION

Sleep soundly

Our rooms are a careful blend of Sapa’s colonial heritage and the colorful cultures that live in the region. Sink into your room after a day of exploring the mountains. Then sleep easy while breathing fresh mountain air before opening the curtains to reveal the views.
