Meetings, Events, Weddings and Parties

At Silk Path we pride ourselves on our events. Whether it’s for corporate teambuilding or it’s a wedding to remember, we can customize the mood and atmosphere with function rooms of different sizes and state-of-the-art equipment. Great food is an important part of any successful get-together, and our culinary teams can match the cuisine with the type of event, including picture-perfect party fare and rewarding meals to punctuate conventions.

Weddings

Our locations are both unique and central, making them prime spots for corporate get-togethers for all teams, whatever the size. We can also be creative based on the purpose of the meeting, whether it's a large annual convention or a decision-making seminar.
