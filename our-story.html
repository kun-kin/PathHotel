Born from passion and inspired by heritage

The Silk Path story starts in 2010 when we opened the Silk Path Hotel Hanoi, the first four-star hotel in the Old Quarter. Dismayed by reports of subpar service in Hanoi’s most compelling neighborhood, we wanted to bring international accommodation standards to the Old Quarter. We soon became one of the most cherished names in Hanoi hospitality.
Five years later we moved forward with a different concept in the same city: The Silk Path Boutique Hanoi. This was Hanoi’s first hotel located right on Hoan Kiem Lake. Here we were able to hone our concept of thoughtful hospitality, offering personalized service and elevating the guest experience.
We followed with our two Grand hotels in two additional heritage cities: The Silk Path Grand Sapa Resort & Spa in 2017 and the Silk Path Grand Hue Hotel & Spa in 2020. Each one embodies the destinations that they’re in. Sapa is home to a colorful tapestry of highland ethnic minority cultures, and our property there is a mountain resort with flower gardens and spectacular views. Hue was once the imperial capital of Vietnam, and our property there is located on a heritage street with views of the city’s sweeping river.
